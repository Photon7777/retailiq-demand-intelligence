# Business Case

Retail companies lose revenue when demand signals, inventory visibility, and business decision workflows are disconnected. Stockouts create missed sales and lower customer trust. Overstock ties up capital and increases markdown risk. Unexplained sales anomalies can hide operational issues, promotion effects, or data quality problems.

RetailIQ demonstrates how a modern data team can build a cloud-native demand intelligence product that connects data engineering, analytics engineering, machine learning, and AI-assisted decision support.

## Value Proposition

- Improve demand visibility across stores and departments.
- Detect inventory risk before customers encounter stockouts.
- Help analysts investigate sales anomalies faster.
- Give business users governed natural-language access to warehouse data.
- Provide a reproducible foundation for dashboards, ML outputs, and AI workflows.
